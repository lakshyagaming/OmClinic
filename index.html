About Om Clinic

Quality Healthcare Close to Home

Om Clinic is a trusted medical practice serving the NCR region and Uttar Pradesh. Located at Main Market, Park, Noida – 201312, we are committed to providing high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors.

Our experienced doctors and friendly staff ensure that every visit is comfortable, efficient, and productive. Whether you need a routine health checkup or management of chronic conditions, Om Clinic is your partner in health.

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Services

We offer a wide range of healthcare services tailored to meet the needs of every family member.

General Consultation
Comprehensive medical consultations for all ages. Diagnose, treat, and manage your health conditions with expert guidance.
Health Checkups
Routine health checkups and preventive screenings to detect potential issues early and keep you at your best.
Fever & Infection
Prompt diagnosis and treatment of fever, viral infections, bacterial infections, and seasonal illnesses.
BP & Diabetes Care
Ongoing management and monitoring of blood pressure, diabetes, and related metabolic conditions with personalized care plans.
Preventive Healthcare
Vaccination programs, lifestyle counseling, and early intervention strategies to keep disease at bay.
Child Healthcare
Specialized care for infants and children — growth monitoring, immunizations, and pediatric consultations.
